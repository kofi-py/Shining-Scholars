About Us

Our Mission

Shining Scholars is dedicated to helping Akyease/Dida Methodist JHS to create a better environment for the students and the staff.

How Did This All Begin?

During our summer vacation in Ghana, we decided to give back to a communtiy. Eventually, God led us to Akeyease/Dida Methodist Junior High School.

What Does The School Need?

The school needs:

more technology setups (e.g. computers),
new furniture (e.g. cabinets, tables, and curtains),
a library,
a place where the teachers can stay,
renovation of the compound,
transportation,
school supplies for the students (e.g. textbooks, pencils, school uniforms, and sandals), and
sanitary pads for women.

Our Plan

We plan to renovate this school by providing:

Infrastructure Development: Raise funds to construct additional classrooms, renovate existing structures, and improve sanitation facilities.
Learning Resources: Organize campaigns to collect and donate books, stationery, and educational materials.
Technology Access: Initiate a tech-drive to acquire computers and establish an internet connection for better digital learning.
Teacher Support: Set up teacher training programs and offer incentives to attract and retain qualified educators.
